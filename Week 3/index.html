Jellyfish Facts

  • Age: Jellyfish have been around for at least 500 million years, making them one of the oldest living creatures on Earth, predating dinosaurs and even trees. 2
  • Survival: They have survived all five mass extinction events, demonstrating their resilience and adaptability to changing environments.
  • Lack of Organs: They do not have brains, hearts, or bones. Instead, they possess a simple nerve net that helps them respond to environmental stimuli. 2
